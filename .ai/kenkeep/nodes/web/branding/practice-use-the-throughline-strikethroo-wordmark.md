The Strikethroo brand mark is the throughline wordmark: the word `strikethroo` in Outfit with tight leading and slight negative letter spacing, plus a horizontal ink strikethrough piercing the lowercase x-height. No colored dot.

Do not use the Dalia UI `<Wordmark>` component for Strikethroo assets. It is a different brand treatment, with Fraunces styling and a colored trailing dot.
